Notes

A collection of expository notes I wrote:
  • During a reading course on expander graphs supervised by Afonso Bandeira, I wrote an exposition about the Alon-Bopanna bound . The bound motivates the definition of Ramanujan graphs and is critical to the theory of expander graphs.

Talks

Talks/presentations that I have given:
  • As part of a reading course, I gave a talk on the method of interlacing polynomials and their application to building bipartite Ramanujan graphs of all degrees to Afonso Bandeira's research group . It was based on a paper by Marcus, Spielman and Srivatsava .
  • I gave a talk at ZUCMAP 2022, where I used examples and a recent application to introduce persistent homology in an intuitive manner to undergraduate students. Toward the end I also (briefly) spoke on persistence modules, graded \(F[t]-\) modules and the existence of persistence barcodes. Here are my (handwritten) slides and a recording.
